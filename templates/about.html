About Us

A gurdwara meaning "the doorway to the guru", is a place of worship for Sikhs, the followers of Sikhism and also a place where weary travellers and pilgrims may rest and eat.
The essential feature of a gurdwara is the presiding presence in it of the holy Sikh Scripture, called the Guru Granth Sahib also referred to as Satguru. This essentially is the focal point of a Gurdwara. The other main component is the holy congregation called the Sadh Sangat who are present within this sacred structure in humble obedience of principles laid down by the Sikh Gurus.



Our Misson

Our goal is to provide a haven in which the community can gather for wholesome social interactions to ultimately promote the Sikh religion. In all aspects of Sikhism including: teaching, language, music, and the philosophies of The Sikh Gurus.
